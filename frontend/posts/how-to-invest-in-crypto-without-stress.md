Stress does not come from crypto itself. It comes from uncertainty, overexposure, and constant decision-making.

## Why crypto feels stressful

- frequent price movement
- information overload
- fear of making mistakes

## How stress is reduced

- fewer decisions
- predefined plans
- realistic expectations
