# Passive Income Through Crypto Staking 2026: Realistic Expectations

Crypto staking is one of the most discussed passive income strategies in digital finance. Understanding realistic expectations versus marketing hype is more important than ever in 2026.

## Realistic APY Ranges in 2026

| Type | Typical APY Range | Risk Level |
|------|-------------------|------------|
| Ethereum native staking | 3-5% | Lower |
| Established DeFi protocols | 5-15% | Medium |
| Structured investment platforms | 8-25% | Medium-Higher |
| Unverified high-yield platforms | 50%+ | Extremely High |

WolvCapital's structured plans range from 8% to 25% APY with all reward distributions recorded on BNB Smart Chain.

## Key Risk Factors

Market risk means crypto asset values can decline, offsetting staking gains. Platform risk exists even on compliant platforms. Liquidity risk means staked assets are locked for the investment duration. Token risk applies when rewards are paid in a pre-listing token.

## Setting Realistic Expectations

Start with a small allocation, use only regulated platforms with verifiable contracts, read all risk disclosures, and understand withdrawal timelines before committing.
