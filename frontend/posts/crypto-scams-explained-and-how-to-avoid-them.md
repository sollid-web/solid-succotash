Scams exploit confusion and urgency, two things beginners often feel.

Education is the strongest defense.

## Common crypto scams

- fake platforms
- impersonation schemes
- unrealistic profit guarantees

## How beginners protect themselves

- verify sources
- avoid pressure
- prioritize transparency
