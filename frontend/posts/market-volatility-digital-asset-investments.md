Volatility is a defining characteristic of cryptocurrency markets. Price movements can be rapid and unpredictable.

Understanding volatility helps investors interpret market behavior more realistically.

## What Causes Volatility?
Factors include:
- Market sentiment
- Global economic events
- Regulatory developments
- Liquidity changes

## Volatility and Investment Planning
Volatility impacts:
- Valuation
- Risk exposure
- Timing decisions

Educational market data sources such as CoinGecko help contextualize price movements.

## Final Thought
Volatility is inherent in digital assets. Awareness and education remain key to responsible participation.
