Many crypto losses happen before an investment even begins, by choosing the wrong platform.

Knowing red flags protects beginners.

## Common red flags

- guaranteed profits
- vague explanations
- pressure tactics
- lack of transparency

## Why beginners are most vulnerable
Inexperience makes it harder to detect warning signs.
