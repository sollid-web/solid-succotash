WolvCapital Referral Program

Overview, eligibility, and compliance summary

Program Overview

Invite new users to WolvCapital and, after they complete qualifying steps and pass manual review, you may receive referral rewards. Rewards are never auto-approved; all financial actions require human review per company policy.

How It Works

  1. Log in and generate your unique referral code from your dashboard.
  2. Share your referral link with friends and colleagues.
  3. When they sign up and become active investors (with admin approval), your referral is recorded.
  4. Eligible rewards are created as pending transactions and require admin approval before payout to your wallet.

Eligibility & Compliance

  • All referrals and rewards are subject to manual compliance review. No guarantees are implied.
  • Self-referrals, fraudulent activity, or abuse void eligibility.
  • WolvCapital may modify reward structures and thresholds as part of its compliance program.
For regulatory reasons, any reward issuance is contingent upon successful verification and administrative approval.

Example Reward Flow

  • Referred user signs up and completes a qualifying deposit.
  • An investment is approved by an admin for the referred user.
  • A referral reward is created and recorded as a pending transaction.
  • After admin approval, the reward posts to your wallet balance.
