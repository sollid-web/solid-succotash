Cryptocurrency markets are highly volatile. Prices may fluctuate rapidly and unpredictably, resulting in potential substantial gains or losses. Market movements can be influenced by regulatory announcements, technological developments, market sentiment, and macroeconomic factors.

The legal and regulatory framework for digital assets is evolving and varies by jurisdiction. Changes in laws, regulations, or enforcement actions may impact platform operations, user access, or the value of digital assets. Future regulatory developments could restrict or prohibit cryptocurrency activities in certain regions.

  • Cybersecurity attacks and data breaches

Certain assets or investment plans may have limited liquidity, affecting your ability to withdraw or transfer funds promptly. Digital asset platforms depend on complex technology infrastructure. Risks include:

  • Platform downtime or technical malfunctions
