Crypto platforms generally fall into two categories: centralized and decentralized.

Each model has distinct characteristics.

## Centralized Platforms
Typically offer:
- Structured user support
- Defined operational processes
- Managed interfaces

## Decentralized Platforms
Often emphasize:
- Peer-to-peer interactions
- User-managed assets
- Smart contract execution

## Choosing What Fits
Investors should assess:
- Comfort with self-management
- Need for support
- Risk tolerance

Understanding platform models supports better alignment with personal investment goals.
