Trust is earned, not promised.

In crypto, trustworthy platforms are transparent, consistent, and user-focused.

## Signs of a trustworthy platform
Look for:

- clear explanations
- visible security practices
- realistic expectations
- responsive support

For an earlier checklist, read [How to Identify Legitimate Crypto Investment Platforms](/blog/identify-legitimate-crypto-investment-platforms).

High returns mean nothing without reliability.
