Trust is built through consistent transparency.

In crypto investment platforms, transparency influences user confidence and long-term engagement.

## Elements of Transparency
- Clear policies
- Open communication
- Accessible support
- Honest risk explanations

## Why It Matters
Transparency reduces confusion and builds realistic expectations.

## Final Thought
Trust is earned through clarity, not promises.
