Crypto is often compared to stocks, but they operate very differently.

Understanding these differences prevents false expectations.

If you are comparing time horizons, see [Short-Term vs Long-Term Crypto Investing](/blog/short-term-vs-long-term-crypto-investing).

## Market structure differences
Crypto:

- trades 24/7
- is global
- is less regulated

Stocks:

- trade limited hours
- are more regulated
- move slower

## Risk and opportunity
Crypto offers:

- higher volatility
- faster innovation
- higher learning curve

Stocks offer:

- stability
- long-term predictability

## Final thoughts
Crypto is not better or worse. It is different. Beginners succeed by respecting those differences.
