# What Is WOLV Token? BEP-20 Crypto on BNB Smart Chain Explained

WOLV is the native BEP-20 token of WolvCapital, a U.S. regulated digital asset investment platform. Every profit distributed on WolvCapital is recorded permanently on the BNB blockchain through WOLV token transactions — verifiable by anyone, anywhere.

## WOLV Token Overview

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Token Name | Wolv Capital |
| Symbol | WOLV |
| Network | BNB Smart Chain |
| Standard | BEP-20 |
| Total Supply | 1,000,000,000 (fixed) |
| Reward Pool | 600,000,000 WOLV (60%) |
| Pre-listing Price | $1.00 (internal reference) |
| DEX Listing | Q3 2026 (PancakeSwap) |

## Token Contract

WOLV is deployed at: `0xe0167279aef7bf4ad313d261da82e8366822270c`

You can verify the contract on [BSCScan](https://bscscan.com/token/0xe0167279aef7bf4ad313d261da82e8366822270c) at any time.

## Tokenomics Breakdown

The 1 billion WOLV supply is fixed — no additional tokens can ever be minted. The allocation is designed to sustain long-term reward distribution:

- **60% (600M WOLV)** — Reward Pool for investor staking returns
- **Remaining 40%** — Platform operations, liquidity provision, and team allocation

## How WOLV Is Earned

When you stake BUSD or USDT through WolvCapital's four investment plans (Pioneer, Vanguard, Horizon, Summit VIP), you receive WOLV tokens proportional to your investment performance. Each distribution is recorded on-chain, creating a transparent, immutable proof of your returns.

## Pre-Listing Phase

WOLV is currently in its pre-listing phase. The $1.00 per WOLV reference price is an internal value set by WolvCapital — it is not yet determined by open market trading.

WOLV rewards earned now are not yet realisable as cash until WOLV is listed on a decentralised exchange. WolvCapital plans to provide initial liquidity at launch to support price stability.

**Important:** Token value after listing may differ from the pre-listing reference price. All investments carry risk.

## DEX Listing: Q3 2026

WolvCapital has scheduled WOLV's listing on PancakeSwap for Q3 2026. At that point, WOLV will be freely tradeable against BNB and USDT, and the market price will be determined by supply and demand.
