Overtrading feels productive, but it often leads to losses.

If you want the psychological root causes, read [The Psychology of Crypto Investing](/blog/psychology-of-crypto-investing).

## Why overtrading happens

- excitement
- fear of missing out
- lack of structure

## How to avoid overtrading

- set clear strategies
- invest long-term
- reduce decision frequency

## Final thoughts
Fewer decisions often lead to better results.
